About

Hello, I am Oleksii Butrin. This photo captures one of the happiest days of my life, my first flight as a co-pilot in a Cessna 172 in 2021. At that time I was working as a project manager at a small Kyiv flight school and flight agency. I also had a humble home business — artisan hot sauces manufacture. The irony of fate is that both parts of my pre-war life — general aviation and entrepreneurship — are gone, at least until the end of the war. So I decided to remember my IT-youth and master the Frontend. With a basic knowledge of HTML and CSS, I began to improve these skills and also learned JavaScript and React in the early months of the war.
